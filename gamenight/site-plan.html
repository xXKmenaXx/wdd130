Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my personal website will be an information website that will provide viewers information on what to expect during a game night hosted by my wife and I. It will help those who desire to join us, understand what they can expect for games and snacks.

Audience

The intended audience would be anyone looking to join us for our next game night so that they can know what to expect when they arrive.

Content

Home page

The Home page will be the page where my viewers will be introduced to the game nights being held. It will consist of a clear inviting message to come and join us for our next game night. On the home page, a calander will be displayed that will show when the next game night is available. This page will display a section where the viewer will be prompted to fill out some information if they are interested in joining a game night that has already been scheduled or for any upcoming available dates. A button will also be displayed that will allow the viewer to RSVP for any given game night as filled out by the prompts above the button.

Images for the Home page

Games page

The Games page will display two sections in which the games will be presented for different options to play. There will be a board game section near the top that will list out different board games that we have available and will give a short description of the game. The discription will include a brief synopsis of the game and details such as how many players can play the game at a time, a complexity rating to show if the game is either complicated or easy to either set up or play, and any recommendations or feedback from previous guests regarding the game. Similar to the board game section, there will be a video game section that will also list out the available games and consoles that may be played. A short description of the game will also be included.

Images for the Games Page

Menu page

On the Menu page, viewers will be able to see the variety of options that can be selected as far as a meal, snacks and drinks go. A short description will be included for all the options such as diner options available to enjoy. There will also describe the option to include complimentary snacks and/or invite guests to bring snacks to share with the rest of the party. Similar to the snacks there will be a description of drinks that are available or that may be brought in to the venue.
