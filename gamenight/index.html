What to Expect

Join us for an unforgettable evening of fun and friendly competition at our next Game Night event. Whether you're a board game enthusiast or looking to discover new favorites, our game nights offers something for everyone. Bring your friends, family, or come solo and make new friends as you enjoy a variety of games in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere.

Find us at 123 Main St., Houston, TX 77084. Mena's Game Nights runs every Saturday from 6:00 PM to 10:00 PM. Admission is free and all are welcome!
